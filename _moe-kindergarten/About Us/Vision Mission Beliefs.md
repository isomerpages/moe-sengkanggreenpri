<p><strong>Our Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>A Strong Start for Every Child</p>
<p><strong>Our Mission</strong>
</p>
<p>To provide quality pre-school education that is affordable to Singaporeans
and catalyse quality improvements in the sector.</p>
<p><strong>Our Beliefs</strong>
</p>
<p>We believe that children are <strong><em>joyful</em></strong>, <strong><em>curious</em></strong>, <strong><em>active </em></strong>and <strong><em>competent </em></strong>learners.</p>
<p>We nurture children holistically, so that they are confident, demonstrate
strong social skills and have a good foundation in literacy and numeracy.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Our Curriculum</strong>
</p>
<p>Our curriculum is specifically designed based on MOE's Nurturing Early
Learnings (NEL) Framework. This is where we believe that children are joyful,
active, curious and competent learners, and we aim to nurture your child
holistically to be lifelong learners. In addition, we believe that children
learn best through an integrated approach and intentionally planned learning
activities.</p>
<p>MK offers classes in the 3 official MTLs (Chinese, Malay and Tamil). We
encourage children of Chinese, Malay and Indian ethnic race to take their
own MTLs.</p>
<p>Our MK curriculum comprises of 2 flagship programmes:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>the HI-Light Programme and;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>the Starlight Literacy Programme for English and MTL.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Each term, the children will also be involved in a Weeks of Wonder project.</p>
<p>In addition, each MK will also have our own centre programme(s), and together,
it makes up our Total MK@Sengkang Green Curriculum.</p>
<p></p>

</p>

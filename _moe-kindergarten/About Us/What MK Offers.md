<p>MK health check: <strong><u>7:50 AM to 8:00 AM</u></strong>
</p>
<p>MK lessons begin promptly at 8:00 AM. It is essential for children to
be in class by this time, as they will start the day with circle time and
greetings, which helps set the tone for the rest of the day.</p>
<p>Please note that <strong><u>any arrival after 8:00 AM will be considered late</u></strong>.</p>
<p>Gate will be opened again at 8:15 AM for latecomers.</p>
<p>Thereafter, should you arrive after this time (we understand that there
might be unforeseen situations), kindly ring the office for assistance.
All teachers are engaged in lessons, thus we seek your kind understanding
that there may be a wait of 10 - 20 minutes before we can attend to you.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>For Full Day Care: MOE Kindergarten (MK) + Kindergarten Care (KCare) Service</strong></h4>
<p>MOE Kindergarten (MK) offers a 4-hour programme. However, parents who
require a full-day service for their children can opt for Kindergarten
Care (KCare) which is provided by EduSMART Learning Hub and based on MOE
guidelines.</p>
<p>KCare provides the time and space for children to play, explore and discover
in a specially designed environment with carefully chosen resources. Your
child will have a variety of play experiences and exposure to different
areas of interest. <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/kindergarten-care" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Click here to find out more about KCare service.</a>
</p>
<p>KCare fees are payable directly to the KCare vendor.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong><u>MK + KCare</u></strong></h4>
<p>From 2026 onwards, the KCare service at MK@Sengkang Green will operate
only with a single KCare session in the afternoon.</p>
<p>During term time when MK is in operation, MK teachers will receive the
children who are in KCare from 7:00 AM and KCare will take over from 12:00
PM to 7:00 PM.</p>
<p>During school holidays or when when MK is closed, KCare will continue
to operate between 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.</p>
<p>Note that KCare centres can close for up to six days, in addition to Teachers'
Day, Children's Day, and gazaetted public holidays, each calendar year.</p>
<p></p>

<p>The Young Philatelists is a newly launched CCA in Sengkang Green Primary
School in partnership with Singapore Post. Philately is the collection
and study of stamps.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Highlights/yp2.jpg">
</div>
<p>Our Young Philatelists will have the opportunity to spark their curiosity
through the study of stamps and philatelic material. Students can research
historical and cultural contexts, learn to pay attention to details, strengthen
their communication skills, and embark on their own self-directed journeys
of discovery that extend beyond the classroom. The CCA aims to nurture
in the Young Philatelists the SKGIAN Learning Dispositions such as <strong>S</strong>ense
of curiosity, <strong>K</strong>nowledge of the world, <strong>G</strong>rit,<strong> I</strong>nventiveness, <strong>A</strong>ppreciation
and e<strong>N</strong>gagement. These learning dispositions will develop
the Young Philatelists to be effective lifelong learners, ready for the
future.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Highlights/yp3.jpg">

<p>The Young Philatelists CCA was officially launched on 5 March 2026 by
our Principal Mr Gau (3rd row, 4th from left) and the Postmaster General,
Ms Neo Su Yin (3rd row, 5th from left) from SingPost.</p>
<p>More information and pictures will be shared as our Young Philatelists
embark on their journey to discover more about the themes depicted in the
postage stamps of Singapore and other countries.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>SENGKANG GREEN PRIMARY SCHOOL<br /></strong><strong>CUB SCOUT UNIT</strong></p>
<p><strong><u>UNIT OVERVIEW<br /></u></strong>Sengkang Green Cub Scout is the only Uniform Group CCA offered in Sengkang Green Primary School. Established in 2018, our unit has grown with 70 active Cub Scout members now.</p>
<p>The Cub Scouts Unit aims to:</p>
<p>1) Develop their leadership qualities to become more confident and competent leaders.<br />2) Enable scouts to recognise strengths, needs, values of others and appreciate diversity in any environment.<br />3) Empower them to make positive contributions to the school and community.</p>
<p>To achieve these aims, our students go through the Cub Scout programme run by our teachers and participate in experiential trips, competitions, leadership camps and courses. They are also given the opportunities to learn and hone outdoor and life skills like map reading and outdoor cooking.</p>
